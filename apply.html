Apply to Coach

Join the team as a volunteer math coach at Brookline middle schools.

Application deadline

May 31, 2025 — submit your application before midnight.

What coaches do

As a Math League coach, you'll volunteer at Brookline middle schools, run math enrichment sessions, and help write and administer the annual town-wide competition. It's a meaningful way to give back to the community and share your love of math.

Who should apply

Any current Brookline High School student is welcome to apply. You don't need a competition math background — just enthusiasm, reliability, and a genuine interest in helping middle schoolers enjoy math.

How to apply

Fill out the application form below. We'll review applications on a rolling basis and reach out with next steps.

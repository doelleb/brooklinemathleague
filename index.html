Who We Are

A community built around math

🏫

Student-Run

BML is entirely organized and operated by students at Brookline High School, from planning practice sessions to writing competition problems.

📐

Coaching Program

Every year we send volunteer high school coaches to Brookline middle schools to run math practice sessions and prepare students for the competition.

🏆

Town-Wide Competition

We host a town-wide math competition open to all Brookline middle schoolers. Our coaches collaborate to write original problems, and we announce individual and school-wide results.

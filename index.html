What we do

About the League

Coaching at Brookline middle schools

Every year, our high school coaches volunteer at Brookline middle schools to run sessions, introduce students to competition math, and build problem-solving skills in a fun environment.

Town-wide competition

We host an annual town-wide math competition for Brookline middle schoolers. All our coaches work together to write the competition questions. Results are announced with overall middle school rankings and recognition for top students.

Join us

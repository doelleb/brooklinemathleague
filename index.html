About

The Brookline Middle School Math League is a student-run organization based at Brookline High School. Every year, we send volunteer BHS coaches to work with students at Brookline middle schools, and we hold a town-wide competition for all Brookline middle schoolers. Our coaches collaborate to write original competition questions, and we announce individual and school-wide results. We are always looking for new volunteers — contact us at brooklinemathleague@gmail.com.
